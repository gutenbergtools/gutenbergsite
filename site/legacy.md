# Our Legacy

The basic mission of Project Gutenberg has always been to preserve and distribute, free of charge, literary works. Over the years, the number of works has grown in an amazing way, and has focused on using the internet to deliver books unrestricted by US copyright laws for free. All along, that work has been made possible by volunteers and by donations by many, many users.

As the collection has grown, the complexity of our software and systems has increased along with those donations, and we no longer depend solely on volunteer labor. We now employ a small but mighty staff who facilitate the work of our many volunteers. 

Although the "preserve" part of our mission requires us to plan for long term finances of the organization, having the sort of reserves that could assure our long term existence seemed out of reach. That is changing. Thanks to a bequest from a long time reader, we have started a modest endowment. In the hope that other folks might be interested in building that endowment into a major source of our operating expenses, we've started a list of "Legacy Donors".
